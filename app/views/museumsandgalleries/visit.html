There is plenty to see and do at our nine attractions. Discover and explore exciting museums, grand country houses, fascinating industrial mills, majestic medieval ruins and an inspiring art gallery.​

  • Abbey House Museum

    A lively, interactive museum which was short-listed for the Guardian ‘Kids in Museum Family Friendly Award’ in 2011.

  • Kirkstall Abbey

    Kirkstall Abbey boast historic architecture amid a haven of wildlife and greenery.

  • Leeds Art Gallery

    Located in the city centre and free of charge, the art gallery offers an inspiring and fun visit for all.

  • Leeds City Museum

    Experience a world of discovery... explore four floors of interactive and exciting galleries.

  • Leeds Discovery Centre

    Home to over a million wonders, Leeds Museums Discovery Centre cares for the city’s collections.​

  • Leeds Industrial Museum at Armley Mills

    Was once the world's largest woollen mill, it houses a wealth of gems located in a beautiful riverside setting.

  • Lotherton Hall

    A charming Edwardian house and country estate once home to the Gascoigne family.

  • Temple Newsam

    A magnificent country mansion set within 1500 acres of beautiful parkland, complete with a rare breed farm.

  • Thwaite Mills Watermill

    Thwaite Mills is one of the last remaining examples in Britain of a water-powered mill.
