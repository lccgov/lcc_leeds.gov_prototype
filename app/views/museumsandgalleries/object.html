Altar

This altar is inscribed with a dedication to the goddess Brigantia by Cingetissa.  Brigantia was a native goddess of the Iron Age Brigantes tribe who inhabited a large part of northern England.  Brigantia was later adopted by the Romans who associated her with Victory or Minerva.  A variety of religions were practised in Roman Britain and altars were frequently dedicated by individuals and set up in temples or as roadside shrines.
