Raising the Participation Age (RPA) is a government measure which calls to young people to participate in learn​ing or training until their 18th birthday.

If you are 16 or 17 and not in education, employment or training, you can visit one of our drop-in centre services to get support from a Leeds Pathways adviser and/or receive sign-posting to other relevant services.

You can also visit the Youth Information Hub for information, advice and guidance on a range of topics including housing, travel and money.​
