Drop-in centre services

If you are 16 or 17 and not in education, employment or training, you can visit one of our drop-in centre services to get support from a Leeds Pathways adviser and/or receive sign-posting to other relevant services.

Grand Futures Leeds

Grand Futures Leeds is a network of arts organisations across the city that are working together to increase the number of arts training and career opportunities available to young people in Leeds.

Developing your skills and experience

If you feel you need to develop further skills or you need more experience to put on your application or CV, try one of the opportunities below.

• Vinspired: Volunteering opportunities

• International Service: Volunteer overseas

• Take a gap year: The Year Out Group

• Take a gap year: Overseas volunteering

• Gap year: Foreign travel advice

• Distance Learning: Open University

• Social Mobility Foundation: Support for high-achieving, ​disadvantaged young people
