Discover the range of full-time, flexible and short courses and alternative provision available in Leeds.

If you are aged 13-17 you can use UCAS Progress to search and apply for post-16 courses across England and Wales. If you are in Year ​11 you ​must make your application using UCAS Progress.
Not all courses are shown on UCAS Progress, so you should still check provider’s websites.

Additional Options from age 14​

Whilst the majority of young people will find that continuing their statutory education is best served within their current school, in Leeds there are two additional options young people can consider for year 10 onwards:

• 14+ Apprenticeship Academy at Leeds City College
• UTC (University Technical College) Leeds

Using the Leeds City Council School Search you can also view a list of all secondary schools in Leeds
