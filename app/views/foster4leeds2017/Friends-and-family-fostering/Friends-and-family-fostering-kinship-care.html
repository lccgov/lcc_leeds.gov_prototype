1. Kinship care

You’re a kinship carer if you’re looking after the child of a relative or friend on a full time basis, either temporarily or permanently.

Sometimes this is done as a private fostering arrangement.

The biggest group of kinship carers are grandparents, but there are many others including brothers and sisters or aunts and uncles. To make sure you’re getting the right support it’s important to let us know about your circumstances.

You can contact our kinship care team on 0113 378 3537.
