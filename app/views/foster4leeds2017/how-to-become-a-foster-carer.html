Information on what happens after you register your interest in becoming a foster carer with us.

I've registered my interested - what happens next?

Once you’ve read about eligibility and registered your interest, you will be guided through the application process. It usually takes about six months.

The steps that we go through while we get to know you and prepare you for fostering are:

  1. A home visit
  2. Completion of an application form
  3. Attending a three day training course
  4. General medical and criminal records check (known as a Disclosure and Barring Service – DBS check)
  5. Working with a social worker on your assessment and portfolio
  6. Attending a fostering panel
  7. Registration as a foster carer

Throughout the whole process you will be offered support, advice and the opportunity to ask questions.
