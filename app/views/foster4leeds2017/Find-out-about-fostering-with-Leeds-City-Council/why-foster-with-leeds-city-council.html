3. Why foster with us?

There are many reasons to foster with Leeds City Council, including financial support, allowances, a comprehensive training package, your own support worker and gym membership.

{% include "includes/foster4leeds2017/panel-quote-alt.html" %}

Training

You’ll have lots of opportunities to access training to develop your skills in supporting children and young people through difficult times.

You can:

  • attend courses with all travel expenses paid
  • complete online training

All these opportunities will work around your childcare commitments.

Financial support

Financial support is made up of two parts, a weekly allowance based on the age of the child and a weekly fee based on your skill level.

The weekly allowance per child is:

Weekly allowance per child
Childs age Amount
0 – 4 years £142.86
5 – 10 years £162.73
11 – 15 years £202.58
16 years plus £246.44

The additional payments for skills fee levels are:

Payment for skills fee level
Level First child Second child Each subsequent child Paid annual leave entitlement
1 N/A N/A N/A N/A
2 £120 £120 £60 10 days
3 £170 £170 £85 10 days
4 £220 £220 £110 14 days

Your skills level will be discussed in detail at the home visit stage and we’ll advise you on how your experience is matched to a level.

The allowances are examples and are updated annually.

Ongoing support

Our fostering teams provide local support groups for foster carers. The support offered includes:

  • your own social worker
  • membership to the Leeds Foster Carer Association
  • membership to the Fostering Network
  • opportunities for you to meet other carers
  • the latest information on our work and the services we provide
  • practical advice and support

More reasons to foster with Leeds City Council

We also offer:

  • free annual Bodyline membership for foster carers
  • free annual junior Bodyline membership for children aged 8 to 18 years
  • free junior swimming lessons
  • discounts to local businesses
  • additional home and contents insurance for our approved foster carers
  • extra allowances for children’s birthdays, Christmas (or another selected religious holiday) and summer school holidays

If you are already a foster carer registered elsewhere you can transfer to Leeds city council.
