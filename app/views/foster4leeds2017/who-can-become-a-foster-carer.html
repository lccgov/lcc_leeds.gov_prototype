Am I eligible to foster?

You don’t need any special qualifications to be a foster carer.

We look for people who:

  • enjoy the challenges of looking after children
  • have a spare bedroom (it doesn’t matter whether you rent, live in a council property or own your own home)
  • live within 20 miles of Leeds city centre
  • are over 21 (if you are between 21 and 24 you will need relevant child care experience)
  • have time to meet a child’s needs
  • don’t have any convictions or cautions against children

Real-life case study video of families who foster in Leeds

Who can apply?

We welcome applications from any ethnic background, religion and sexuality. It doesn’t matter whether you are employed, unemployed, married, single or divorced. The important thing is that you have time to give to a child.

There is no such thing as a typical foster carer and we assess all applications on an individual basis. Please get in touch if you are interested in fostering or have any questions.
