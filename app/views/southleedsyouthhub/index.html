South Leeds Youth Hub is the Leeds Myplace centre, a safe space for young people in south Leeds to take part in affordable activities outside school hours. There are opportunities to join a youth club, try something new, develop new skills, have fun, and make friends, all in a state of the art building.

There is everything from a high-tech music and film studio, a top-quality bike mechanics ​workshop, performance hall, and flexible spaces for creative and performing arts, teaching and learning, meeting rooms, and a welcome area. Some of these spaces are available for venue hire , and during school hours the building Southway Alternative Education operates from here too.

At South Leeds Youth Hub we are lucky to work with some brilliant local partners and supporters. Under the direction of young people from south Leeds, we are constantly evolving and developing to make sure that local young people have what they want, need and value.
