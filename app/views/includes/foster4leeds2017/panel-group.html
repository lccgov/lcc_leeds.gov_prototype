What is fostering?

Fostering is when children who aren’t able to live with their birth families are placed in a safe and loving family home on a temporary basis.

Find out more about what fostering is

Why foster with us?

There are many reasons to foster with Leeds City Council, including financial support, allowances, a comprehensive training package, your own support worker and gym membership.

Find out more about fostering with is

Who can be a foster carer?

You don’t need any special qualifications to be a foster carer.

Find out more about who can be a foster carer

How to become a foster carer

Once you’ve registered your interest you will be guided through the application process. This usually takes about six months.

Find out more about how to become a foster carer

Who can I foster?

Children and young people of all ages need foster carers and we’ll ask you if you have an age preference as part of your assessment.
